Introduction

Zhuang Song ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app "Lumina" (the "App") handles your information.

Data Collection

The App operates primarily with on-device processing. We do not store your personal photos or voice recordings on our servers permanently.

  • Images: Photos you upload for AI 3D model generation are transmitted securely to third-party AI providers (SiliconFlow, Tripo AI) solely for the purpose of generating the content.
  • Voice: Voice data is processed to generate visual effects and is not recorded or stored.
