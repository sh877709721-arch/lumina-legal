Lumina App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Zhuang Song ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app "Lumina" (the "App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

2.1 AI Generation Data

  • Images: Photos you upload for "Image to 3D" or "Emotion Portrait" features are transmitted securely to our third-party AI providers solely for the purpose of generating the artwork. We do not use these images for model training or other unauthorized purposes.
  • Voice: Voice inputs used in the "Resonance" feature are processed to generate visual effects or emotional analysis. We do not permanently store your raw voice recordings on our servers.

2.2 Health Data (HealthKit)

Lumina integrates with Apple HealthKit to read your Heart Rate data for stress analysis and to write Mindful Minutes to record your relaxation sessions.

  • We do not use your health data for advertising, marketing, or use-based data mining.
  • We do not share your health data with any third parties, except for storing it securely in your own iCloud container if synchronization is enabled.
  • Health data processing happens locally on your device or within the secure Apple ecosystem.

2.3 Cloud Synchronization

The App uses iCloud (CloudKit) to synchronize your membership status, credits, and generated assets across your devices. This data is stored in your private iCloud container, which is managed by Apple.

3. Third-Party AI Service Disclaimer

Our App integrates Artificial Intelligence Generated Content (AIGC) features. When using these functions, please be aware of the following data processing rules:

  • Data Transmission: To enable features such as intelligent dialogue, image generation, and 3D model construction, the text prompts, conversation content, and reference images you input/upload are transmitted to third-party AI service providers via encrypted channels.
  • Authorized Service Providers:
    • SiliconFlow / DeepSeek: Used for natural language processing, sentiment analysis, and copywriting generation.
    • Meshy AI / Tripo AI: Used for generating 3D models based on uploaded images.
  • Data Usage Restrictions: Your data is used strictly for the purpose of completing the specific generation task or analysis request. We have confirmed with the aforementioned providers that your data will not be used to train their general AI models, nor will it be disclosed or sold.
  • PII Protection: We do not transmit your Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as your name, phone number, or precise geographic location, to AI service providers.
  • User Control: You have the right to opt-out of using AI features. Upon the first launch of the App, we will request your explicit authorization. 4. Subscriptions and Payments

All financial transactions (subscriptions and credit purchases) are handled securely by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store your credit card information.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your generated content only as long as necessary to provide the service. You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time via the "Delete Account" option in the App's settings (User Profile).
